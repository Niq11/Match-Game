How to Play

The objective of the game is to match pairs of cards. Here's how to play:

  • 1. Click on a card to flip it over and reveal the image.
  • 2. Click on another card to try and find the matching image.
  • 3. If the cards match, they will remain face up. If they don't match, they will flip back over.
  • 4. Continue flipping cards to find all the matching pairs.
  • 5. The game is complete when all pairs have been matched.
